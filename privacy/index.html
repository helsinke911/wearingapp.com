Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address, name, and clothing photos you upload to the app. We also collect your device location to provide weather-based outfit recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Wearing App service — organizing your wardrobe and generating outfit suggestions. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Clothing photos are stored as compressed images and are only accessible by you.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Remove.bg for background removal and OpenWeatherMap for weather data. These services receive only the minimum data needed to function.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen inside the app.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: contact@wearingapp.com
